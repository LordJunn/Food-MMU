Goreng-goreng

Maggi Goreng RM 5.00

Initial comments:
19/12/2022 - Meat, 2x cucumber & some lime? Worth.

For context, HTC's maggi goreng doesn't contain meat, and last I had it it was only with egg.
This one on the other hand not only has chicken meat (just 1 piece though), 2x cucumber slices AND lime for taste. Which to me, makes it worth more than HTC even though it's 50 cents more expensive.

Nasi Goreng Kampung RM 6.00

Initial comments:
20/12/2022 - on God this is better than Komatra's one. Consistency is yet to be tested.

The fried garlic is free of charge. Self service for the win.
Taste wise, it's more flavourful vs Komatra, or maybe even HTC, though I mostly forgotten how it was.
More meat and anchovies to end the meal, or it was just me being "rational" on the sides and the rice.
